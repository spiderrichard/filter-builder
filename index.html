How to use Filter Builder

  1. In the tables above Select all the criteria that you want to either include or exclude form your search.
  2. Copy your filter using the Copy button in the Filter Bar.
  3. Pastes your filter into the pokemon search bar in Pokemon Go to show all that meet the filter criteria.
